About Our Workshop

Established in 2005, Shafiq Engineering Works is a small local workshop based in Faisalabad, Pakistan. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality spare parts for industrial machines including loom machines and feeder machines.

Our mission is to expand our reach into national and international industrial markets by providing exceptional quality products that meet global standards.

With a team of skilled engineers and technicians, we ensure that each spare part is crafted with precision and undergoes rigorous quality checks before delivery.
